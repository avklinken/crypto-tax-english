When you gift cryptocurrency, you may avoid paying taxes on the transaction itself, but there are several nuances to consider.</p><h3>The Annual Gift Exclusion</h3><p>One of the critical factors in gifting cryptocurrency tax-free is the annual gift exclusion. As of 2023, you can gift up to $16,000 per recipient each year without incurring gift tax. This exclusion applies to both fiat currency and cryptocurrency, allowing you to transfer digital assets within this limit tax-free.</p><h2>When Does Gifting Trigger Taxes?</h2><p>While gifting cryptocurrency up to the annual exclusion is tax-free, exceeding this limit might trigger the gift tax.
