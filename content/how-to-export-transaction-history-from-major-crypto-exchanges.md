<h2>How to Export from Binance</h2>
<h3>Step-by-Step Guide</h3>
<ol>
<li>Log in to your Binance account and navigate to the <strong>Wallet</strong> section.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Transaction History</strong> from the dropdown menu.</li>
<li>Choose the type of transactions you want to export: deposits, withdrawals, or trades.</li>
<li>Select the date range for the transactions you wish to export.</li>
<li>Click the <strong>Export</strong> button to download the transaction history as a CSV file.</li>
</ol>

<h2>Exporting from Coinbase</h2>
<h3>Comprehensive Steps</h3>
<ol>
<li>Sign in to your Coinbase account and go to <strong>Reports</strong>.</li>
<li>Select <strong>Generate Report</strong> and choose the type of report you need.</li>
<li>Pick the desired date range and transaction type.</li>
<li>Click on <strong>Generate Report</strong> to create a downloadable CSV file.</li>
</ol>
<p>Once you have exported your data, you can easily integrate it with tax software such as <strong><a href='https://koinly.io/?via=ED1CED45&utm_source=affiliate' target='_blank' rel='noopener nofollow sponsored'>Koinly</a></strong> to streamline your tax reporting process.</p>

<h2>Exporting from Kraken</h2>
<h3>Detailed Instructions</h3>
<ol>
<li>Log in to Kraken and click on <strong>History</strong> in the navigation menu.</li>
<li>Choose <strong>Export</strong> from the options available.</li>
<li>Select the type of data you want to export and specify the time frame.</li>
<li>Click <strong>Submit</strong> to download your transaction history in CSV format.</li>
</ol>
